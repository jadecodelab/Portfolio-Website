## Problem

Clinic booking flows need to make appointment choices clear while preventing duplicate or confusing submissions.

## Build

The app lets patients choose a service, select from preset appointment slots, and persist bookings with a lightweight backend.

## What I Practiced

- Building backend routes with Flask
- Modeling appointment data with SQLite
- Creating a focused user flow from selection to confirmation
