## Problem

Conversational AI systems can sound polished during a demo but fail when real callers interrupt, ask unclear questions, or move through a workflow out of order.

## Build

This project simulates patient calls, records the conversation flow, and helps surface bugs and edge cases that would be hard to catch with manual testing alone.

## What I Practiced

- Designing repeatable test scenarios
- Thinking through real-world voice bot failure modes
- Connecting backend automation with external communication APIs
