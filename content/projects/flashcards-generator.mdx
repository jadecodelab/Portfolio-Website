## Problem

Students often have notes but need a faster way to turn them into active recall practice.

## Build

The app generates flashcards from pasted notes or uploaded files, supports review mode, shuffling, and locally saved decks.

## What I Practiced

- Connecting a frontend workflow to an AI API
- Designing a useful review interaction
- Handling generated content in a study-focused interface
